Privacy Policy for Currency Trend

Effective date: 2026-06-23

Last updated: 2026-06-23

This Privacy Policy applies to Currency Trend (package name: com.currencytrend.app) and describes how data is processed when you use the app.

We apply a strict data-minimization approach: we do not require account registration, and we do not intentionally collect personal information through the app.

1. Data Controller

For privacy inquiries regarding this app, contact: support@ctrend.app.

2. Information We Process

We do not intentionally collect or store personal data such as name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, payment data, or government identifiers.

To provide exchange-rate features, the app sends currency query requests to third-party rate providers. Standard technical metadata (such as IP address, device/network metadata, and request timestamps) may be processed by those providers as part of normal internet communication.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Retrieve current and historical exchange-rate data requested by the user.
  • Display charts, tables, and calculated trend summaries in-app.
  • Maintain app reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

We do not use your data for advertising profiling or data brokerage.

4. Legal Basis (where applicable)

Where privacy laws require a legal basis (for example GDPR/UK GDPR), processing is based on:

  • Performance of a service: delivering requested currency data.
  • Legitimate interests: app security, reliability, and troubleshooting.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Limited technical request data may be processed by third-party exchange-rate API providers and infrastructure vendors strictly for service delivery and operational security.

6. Third-Party Services

The app depends on external exchange-rate services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own terms and policies. We recommend reviewing third-party privacy terms before use.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain user-account databases for this app. App-local cache is kept only as needed for functionality and may be removed by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards intended to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission, storage, or processing can be guaranteed 100% secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Because third-party infrastructure may operate globally, technical request data may be processed outside your country of residence.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, objection, restriction, portability, or complaint to a supervisory authority. To make a request, contact support@ctrend.app.

11. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age under applicable law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy periodically for legal, technical, or operational reasons. Updates will be posted at the same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: support@ctrend.app

This policy is provided for app-store compliance and user transparency and should be reviewed with legal counsel if required for your jurisdiction.
